About Me

I am a Fullstack Python & Django Developer based in Pokhara. I specialize in developing scalable backend systems and end-to-end web solutions, bridging the gap between complex business logic and clean, deployable code.

My technical expertise is rooted in the Django framework, where I have a proven track record of building production-ready platforms. Most notably, I engineered a full-stack service platform for Little Heart Pet Shop—integrating a MySQL database and a responsive Bootstrap frontend—which has successfully processed 300+ real-world bookings to date.

Beyond web platforms, I developed and deployed the Xdezo Telegram Bot to automate student enrollment and data tracking. I also built InformaticsCafeteria, a full-stack web application designed to eliminate long queues by allowing students to order online. This solution optimizes limited break times for students while providing the cafeteria with robust tools for order management and inventory tracking.

I hold a BSc (Hons) in Computing from London Metropolitan University, which provided a rigorous foundation in software systems. Building on this, my professional experience has given me hands-on expertise in cPanel hosting and live environment maintenance—ensuring smooth deployment and performance optimization for real-world business applications.
