Visual Identity & Social Media

Little Heart
Pet Shop.

Cultivating trust and warmth for Pokhara’s premier pet grooming salon through cohesive seasonal storytelling and clear, functional service design.

01 — The Brief

Professionalizing Compassion.

Little Heart Pet Shop needed a social media presence that reflected their high standards of care. The challenge was to transform a complex grooming price list into an easily digestible mobile format while maintaining a warm, approachable brand voice during festive seasons.

02 — The Strategy

Heart to Paws Connection.

The goal was to professionalize the brand's social presence to drive bookings. We needed to balance emotional engagement with functional clarity.

The visual strategy utilized a warm, "golden" color palette to evoke comfort, paired with high-quality imagery of happy pets dressed for the occasion, instantly humanizing the brand.

03 — Style Guide

Visual DNA.

I selected a "Golden-Hour" palette to evoke feelings of warmth, safety, and domestic comfort. This was paired with a geometric sans-serif for clarity in information-heavy grooming menus.

05 — UX Architecture

Logical Hierarchy.

For the grooming menu, I implemented an Information Architecture that prioritizes the most frequently asked questions: "What is the service?" and "How much for my dog's size?"

  • Iconography: Simplified dog silhouettes for instant size recognition.
  • Typography: Bold pricing for scanning ease.
  • Visual Cues: Seasonal themes (Tihar/Christmas) to maintain high engagement.
06 — Functional Clarity

Simplifying the Complex.

Pet grooming pricing is complicated. The challenge was to present variables (size, weight, service type) in a way that was instantly understandable on a small screen.

I developed a clear visual hierarchy using distinct headers, bullet points for deliverables, and iconography for dog sizes, making the booking decision effortless for owners.

07 — The Result

Measurable Impact.

The redesign led to a 40% reduction in pricing-related inquiries on Instagram DM, as customers could now self-serve information. The festive designs achieved record engagement rates, strengthening Little Heart's position as Pokhara's community leader in pet care.
