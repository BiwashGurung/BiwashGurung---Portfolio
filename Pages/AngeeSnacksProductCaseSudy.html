02 — Behavioral Engagement

The "Treat" Logic.

I architected a high-engagement digital campaign titled "Who Gets the Treat?" This interactive system uses photo-manipulation to break the standard grid, creating a 3D visual hierarchy that captures attention in fast-scrolling environments.

By pitting cats against dogs in a playful "selection" format, the design drove community interaction and reinforced the brand's dual-market appeal.
