FMCG Product

Hello Charcoal Toothpaste

The Insight

Everyday oral care products need to break out of the sterile, clinical aesthetic to appeal to modern consumers looking for natural, bold alternatives.

The Execution

A dark, textured charcoal background immediately communicates the active ingredient. The stark white graphic stripes provide a dynamic backdrop, making the matte black tube and vibrant green mint leaves pop to emphasize freshness and purity.

Consumer Electronics

Apple AirPods Max

The Insight

High-end audio equipment is a lifestyle statement. The marketing must convey not just technical specifications, but the immersive, emotional experience of the music itself.

The Execution

A soft, premium monochromatic blue palette creates a calming, luxurious atmosphere. Integrating a realistic UI overlay of a currently playing track bridges the gap between hardware and the user experience, supported by minimalist, clean spec icons below.

Gaming Hardware

PS5 DualSense Edge

The Insight

Premium gaming accessories are sold on the promise of elevated performance and breaking boundaries. The visual needs to feel expansive, limitless, and powerful.

The Execution

Utilizing a bright sky background contrasts heavily with the bold, heavy typography anchored in the lower navy block. Allowing the controller to literally break out of the frame adds depth, tangibility, and a sense of unleashed potential.
