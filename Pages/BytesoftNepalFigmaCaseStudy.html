Agency Redesign 2025

Bytesoft Nepal

Modernizing the digital identity of Nepal's leading IT firm.

The Challenge

Bytesoft Nepal needed a digital home that matched their reputation for agility and enterprise-grade quality. The old structure was content-heavy, and we needed to make their 250+ successful projects the star of the show while simplifying the user journey.

The Solution

I implemented a high-contrast, grid-based layout that prioritizes 'Trust' through client testimonials and 'Clarity' through a redesigned service architecture. The final design focuses on a "Mobile-First" approach, ensuring the agency looks professional on any device.

My Role

UI/UX & Visual Designer

Project Type

Agency Web Platform

Auto-Layout, Components, Style Guides.

Design System

Consistency & Scalability

To ensure the brand felt cohesive, I developed a strict design system. I chose Inter for its technical clarity and a high-energy Electric Cyan to represent innovation against a professional deep-charcoal background.

Final Deliverables

High-Fidelity Interface

Responsive Design

Built for Every Screen

Over 60% of Bytesoft's traffic comes from mobile. I ensured every component—from the service cards to the complex footer—was optimized for thumb-friendly navigation and fast loading.

Behind the Scenes

Technical Execution

The project was built using Figma's advanced features, including Auto-Layout 5.0, Variable Color Modes, and Interactive Components to ensure a seamless handoff to developers.
