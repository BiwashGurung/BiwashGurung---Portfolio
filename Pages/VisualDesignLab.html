02 — Editorial Design

The Why:

To study the relationship between bold type and high-contrast portraiture.

The Process:

Integrated the headline directly into the subject's silhouette through precise layer masking, creating a sophisticated "peek-through" effect.

Result:

A clean, minimal magazine cover that communicates a powerful narrative through layout alone.
