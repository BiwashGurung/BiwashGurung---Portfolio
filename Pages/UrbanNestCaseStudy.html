Urban Nest

Flexible Urban Living. Community Driven. Community Focused.

The Strategic Brief

Urban Nest addresses the housing needs of young professionals and digital nomads. The goal was to synthesize modern urban speed with the warmth of a community-focused home.

Community Focused Modern Living
01. Design Objective

Trust & Reliability

The identity positions Urban Nest as a smart housing solution, balancing a minimal aesthetic with warm, inviting elements to foster trust in a flexible rental market.

02. Visual Anatomy

The "U" Frame: Represents the Urban landscape and a Universal sense of belonging.

The Nest: The central house icon provides the psychological anchor of "Home" and comfort.

The "N": Embedded within the house, representing the "Nest" and the "Network" of the community.

03. Color & Type

A terracotta accent conveys warmth and brick-and-mortar stability, while the dark navy typography ensures a professional, reliable foundation.
