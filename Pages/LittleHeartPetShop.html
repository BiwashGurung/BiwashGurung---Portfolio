01. The Challenge

Humanizing the
Pet Industry.

The pet industry is often split between cold clinical aesthetics and cluttered discount branding. Little Heart Pet Shop needed a visual language that felt both high-end (premium grooming) and deeply emotional (rescue and care).

Client

Little Heart Pet Shop

Service

Brand Strategy, Logo, Packaging

Year

2025

Location

Pokhara, Nepal

02. Visual DNA

Color & Typography

Fredoka

Why Fredoka? The rounded geometry removes all sharp edges, mimicking the softness of a pet. It denotes safety and approachability.

Playfair

Why Playfair? To maintain a "Premium" status. It adds an editorial elegance that suggests high-quality service and expertise.

Terracotta (#E89B66)

Warmth, energy, and the joy of companionship.

Chocolate (#5D3A1A)

Grounded trust, organic quality, and reliability.

Poppy Red (#E63946)

The literal 'heart'—the pulse of the brand.

04. Execution

Tangible Touchpoints

Retail Packaging

Premium Paper Hand Bag

Designed to carry the brand beyond the shop floor. We chose eco-friendly, reinforced paper to reflect a commitment to nature and quality.

Business Cards

Professional Identity

A tactile introduction to the brand. High-weight stock with a matte finish ensures the "Little Heart" seal feels like a mark of trust.

Point of Sale

Premium Price Tagging

Refined retail labels that balance the "Little Heart" emotional mark with necessary commercial information for a seamless shopping experience.

The Achievement

More than a Mark.
A Brand Legacy.

By synthesizing mathematical grid logic with the emotional warmth of the "Trinity of Care," the Little Heart redesign achieved a unified brand ecosystem. The shop successfully transitioned from a local provider to a premium boutique destination.

Visual Unity 100% consistency across digital and print tactile media.
Market Shift Repositioned as a high-end boutique pet sanctuary.
Scalability Future-proofed for expansion into grooming and apparel.
